Click on the calendar icon in the top right to select specific calendars. This way you can look at only Blue (queer specific) events.
Click on "Month" in the top right to select between week, month, and schedule calendar views. Schedule view allows you to read the complete event descriptions.

Blue - queer specific events or events run by queer organizations
Green - outdoorsy stuff (gardening, hiking, etc.)
Purple - random events (bike maintinence 101, virtual power of attorney clinic, lunar new year celebration, etc.)
Peach - activism (mutual aid, marches, boycots, unions, voting, etc.)
Grey - queer holidays
Black - national holidays
